About Us

Welcome to The Reading! This webpage is by book lovers, for book lovers all over the world. Here you can read others and create your own reviews as well as edit and delete them. Have fun discovering new books, authors and genres as you traverse through The Reading and recommend it to your friends. The more users there are, the more reviews we can share together. Hope you enjoy this webpage and if you have any questions don’t hesitate to contact us through any of our social media or our email:
thereading@readerz.null

CurrentTerms

  1. Do not share personal or sensitive information (your own or others) on The Reading. This infers usernames, passwords and reviews. The Reading will not take responsibility if personal information is leaked.
  2. No hateful or discriminating content.
  3. Inability to comply with current terms will lead to erasure of account and reviews.

Privacy

As stated in terms, The Reading does not wish to and will not store personal information. Registered users can at any time delete their account in the profile section. After deletion The Reading will not save any information about that account. Note that the reviews you have created will remain even after your account’s deletion. If you do not want the reviews to stay on the webpage then delete the reviews before you delete your account.

FAQ

  1. How do I create an account?

    2. Press the 'Register' button in the navigation bar. The type in a username and password of your choice and lastly the 'Register' button. And you are all set! You can start making reviews straight away and also see others reviews.

  2. How do I edit my review?

    3. Press the home button and find your review or press the profile button. On the review there should be two buttons, one blue and one red button. Press the blue button that says 'Edit', change want you want to change in the text form and then press the green button that says 'Add Changes' and voilà! You have successfully edited your review.

  3. How do I delete a review?

    4. Press the 'Profile' button in the navigation bar. And in the first column you will see a button called 'Delete Account'. Press this button to delete your account. Caution! This acction can not be recalled, once you have removed your account you cannot get it back.

  4. Can I edit or delte other users reviews?

    5. No, you can only edit or delete your own reviews. If there is a review that goes agaings The Readings terms and policies, please conntact The Reading and we will remove that review or ask the user to edit the reivew for it to comply wtih current terms and policy.
